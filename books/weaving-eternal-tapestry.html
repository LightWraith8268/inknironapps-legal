The Weaving of Eternal Tapestry

A creation myth — love, loss, and the weaving of spacetime.

Before time, there was the Silence. Before space, there was the Void. In the endless gray, two cosmic beings — Chronos and Cosmos — dream a universe into existence. Their love is the music of creation, their dance the weaving of spacetime. From their touch, galaxies are born and stars are lit.

But their magnificent creation is fragile, a beautiful tapestry of light and shadow that threatens to unravel under its own weight. To save their creation, and all the souls that will ever inhabit it, they must make an impossible choice — a sacrifice that will forever bind them to the fate of the universe and change the very nature of their love.

The Weaving of Eternal Tapestry is a breathtaking creation myth — a story of love, loss, and the ultimate sacrifice. A journey into the heart of creation, a story that will change the way you look at the stars.
