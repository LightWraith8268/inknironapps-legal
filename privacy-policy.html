Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to all mobile applications published by InkNIron Apps ("we", "us", "our"), including but not limited to MatCalc, LibraryIQ, and any future applications distributed under the InkNIron Apps brand.

Information We Collect

Our apps are designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Specifically:

  • No personal data collection — We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information.
  • No account required — Our apps do not require you to create an account or sign in.
  • No analytics or tracking — We do not use third-party analytics services, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies.
  • No network requests — Our apps function entirely offline. No data is sent to or received from external servers.

Data Stored on Your Device

Some of our apps store data locally on your device to provide functionality such as calculation history, user preferences, and custom settings. This data:

  • Is stored only on your device
  • Is never transmitted to us or any third party
  • Can be deleted by clearing the app's data or uninstalling the app
  • May include calculation inputs, results, and user-configured settings

Third-Party Services

Our apps do not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data. We do not use:

  • Advertising networks
  • Analytics platforms
  • Social media integrations
  • Crash reporting services that collect personal data

Children's Privacy

Our apps do not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Our apps are safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
