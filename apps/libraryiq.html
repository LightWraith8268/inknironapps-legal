LibraryIQ

Scan, catalog, and organize your personal book library with AI-powered lookup.

LibraryIQ is the smartest way to catalog, organize, and track your personal book library. Scan a barcode, snap a cover photo, or search by title — LibraryIQ finds your book's metadata from 16+ sources and adds it to your library in seconds.

Scan & add books instantly

Point your camera at any book's barcode to instantly look up its title, author, cover, description, page count, publisher, and more. No manual entry needed. LibraryIQ searches Google Books, Open Library, Hardcover, Amazon, Barnes & Noble, Apple Books, and 10 other sources to find the most complete metadata for every book.

AI-powered cover recognition (AI Enhanced)

Can't find the barcode? Just take a photo of the book's cover. LibraryIQ uses AI to read the title and author from stylized fonts, artistic typography, and non-standard layouts that traditional OCR can't handle.

Smart genre classification (AI Enhanced)

Every book gets a specific, useful genre — not just "Fiction." LibraryIQ identifies subgenres like Contemporary Romance, Epic Fantasy, Psychological Thriller, Romantasy, Dark Romance, and 100+ others.

Organize with collections

Create custom collections to group your books however you want. Reorder collections and books within them. Smart Collections (AI Enhanced) automatically populate based on rules you define — like "all 5-star fantasy books" or "everything by this author."

Track your reading

Mark books as unread, reading, or read. Rate and review your books. Track reading progress with current page. View stats — books read this year, top authors, top genres, average rating, and more.

Multiple views

Switch between list and grid views. Adjust grid size from 2 to 5 columns. Sort by title, author, date added, rating, or status. Group by author, genre, series, status, or rating. Search across your entire library instantly.

Series detection (AI Enhanced)

LibraryIQ automatically detects when a book is part of a series and shows the series name and book number.

Cloud sync & shared libraries (LibraryIQ Pro)

Sync your library across multiple devices with cloud backup. Create a shared library and invite family or friends with a simple 6-character code. Everyone sees the same book catalog, but reading status, ratings, and collections stay private to each person.

Subscription tiers

  • Free — Local library, barcode scanning, full API metadata, manual collections, reading tracking, and stats.
  • AI Enhanced ($1.99/mo or $14.99/yr) — AI genre classification with specific subgenres, AI cover recognition, AI series detection, and smart collections with rule-based filtering.
  • LibraryIQ Pro ($4.99/mo or $39.99/yr) — Everything in AI Enhanced plus cloud sync across devices, shared libraries, and automatic cloud backups.

Privacy first

Your book data stays on your device unless you choose to enable cloud sync. No ads. No tracking. No analytics. We never sell your data.

Export your data

Export your entire library as a CSV file anytime. Your data is yours.
