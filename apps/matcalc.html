MatCalc

Calculate material coverage, conversions, and turf installation needs.

MatCalc is a fast, offline material calculator built for landscapers, contractors, DIYers, and anyone working with bulk materials. Get accurate estimates for coverage, tonnage, cubic yardage, and artificial turf needs — all in one app with no internet connection required.

Coverage calculator

Calculate area and material needs for any project. Choose from 15 shapes — Rectangle, Square, Triangle, Circle, Half Circle, Quarter Circle, Oval, Annulus, Trapezoid, Parallelogram, Rhombus, Pentagon, Hexagon, Octagon, and L-Shape. Enter dimensions in feet and inches for precision. Pick from 12 preloaded materials across rounded rock, angular rock, specialty stone, base materials, and soil and mulch. Results show square footage, cubic feet, tonnage ranges, and cubic yard estimates with settling adjustment.

Gallons to cubic yards converter

Convert US gallons to cubic yards in a single tap. Always rounds up to two decimal places so you never underestimate. Single input, one number out, designed for on-site use.

Turf calculator

Plan artificial turf installations with accurate nail and infill estimates. Enter length and width and get total square footage, perimeter and seam and grid nail counts, number of nail boxes, infill weight in pounds, and number of infill bags. All settings are adjustable — nail spacing, grid spacing, nails per box, infill rate, bag weight, and roll width.

Built for the field

  • Works completely offline — no internet required
  • Customizable materials with custom coverage rates
  • Calculation history kept for 30 days, tap to reload any entry
  • Dark and light themes follow device settings
  • Clean interface designed for one-handed use
  • Ranges that account for real-world settling and variation
