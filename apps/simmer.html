Simmer

Collect, modify, and shop from your recipes with AI-powered imports.

Simmer is a personal recipe manager built for people who actually cook. Collect recipes from anywhere, make them your own, and turn any meal plan into a smart shopping list — all without an account or the cloud.

Collect recipes from anywhere

  • Type it in manually with a clean, distraction-free editor
  • Paste a URL and Simmer fetches the recipe automatically using schema.org structured data — with an AI fallback for sites that don't play nicely
  • Snap a photo of a recipe card, handwritten note, or cookbook page and let AI vision extract the details for you

Make recipes your own

  • Edit any recipe: adjust servings, swap ingredients, rewrite steps
  • Add personal notes, ratings, and tags to keep your collection organized
  • Track nutritional info, prep time, cook time, and difficulty

Smart shopping lists

  • Add any recipe to your shopping list in one tap
  • Simmer merges duplicate ingredients automatically — no more "2 cups flour" appearing five times
  • Check off items as you shop; the list updates in real time

AI that works your way

Simmer supports multiple AI providers so you choose what fits your setup:

  • OpenAI (GPT-4o and later models)
  • Google Gemini
  • Anthropic Claude

Bring your own API key — your key, your data, your cost.

Privacy first

  • No account required — open the app and start cooking
  • All your recipes are stored locally on your device
  • Nothing is sent to our servers; AI calls go directly from your device to your chosen provider

Designed for daily use

  • Clean dark and light themes that look great in any kitchen lighting
  • Fast, fluid navigation built with Flutter
  • Works offline — your recipes are always available, even without a connection

Coming soon

  • Share recipes with friends and family
  • Community recipe discovery

Whether you're recreating grandma's lasagna or experimenting with a new technique, Simmer keeps everything in one place — organized, editable, and ready to shop from. No subscription. No ads. No account. Just cooking.
