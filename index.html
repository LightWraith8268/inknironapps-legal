One person, two crafts.

I'm Riley — I write under Riley E. Antrobus and publish software under Ink & Iron Apps. Same workshop, two outputs: stories that take their time, and tools that don't waste yours.

"Crafted stories. Forged software."

The books and the apps live on the same site because they come from the same hands — but they don't share pages, and they aren't sold next to each other. A novel isn't a product line, and an app isn't a chapter.
